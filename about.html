About

Using open-source data and applications DataLensDC presents readers with insight into the District's character, trends and hacks. The website was created in the summer of 2015 by Kate Rabinowitz, lover of all things data and District.

DataLensDC has been featured in The Washingtonian, The Atlantic's CityLab, Washington City Paper, WJLA ABC 7 News, and more.

Interested in contributing, collaborating or coffee? Email us at datalensdc@gmail.com.
